The Gap

Your clinic closes.
Students don't.

Most campus health services operate weekday hours only — and run at capacity before lunch. After-hours, students rely on expensive emergency rooms or wait until morning. Residence staff make medical decisions they were never trained for.

1

Clinics close at 17:00

The hours students most need care — evenings, weekends, exam periods — are the hours they're left without it.

2

Residence staff carry clinical risk

Sub-wardens face after-hours health incidents with no medical training and no clinical support.

3

Students wait — or go to emergency rooms

Both outcomes are bad. One delays care. The other costs money most students don't have.

4

Unverified sick notes are endemic

Faculty receive certificates from unknown sources with no way to verify their clinical legitimacy.

The Solution

CampusCare+ doesn't replace your clinic.
It extends it.

A WhatsApp-first telemedicine layer that sits over your existing healthcare infrastructure — filling the after-hours gap, supporting residence staff, and giving students access to qualified doctors from the platform they already live in.

WhatsApp Consultations

No app. No login. Students initiate care from WhatsApp, removing every barrier to accessing help.

Qualified Doctors, Daily

Registered medical doctors available 09:00–22:00, including weekends and university holidays.

Digital Prescriptions

Issued after a genuine clinical consult and routed directly to partner pharmacies.

QR-Verified Certificates

Every medical certificate carries a QR code scannable by any smartphone. Fake sick notes end here.

Residence DOC Kits

SAHPRA-approved telemedicine kits — including stethoscope, vitalometer, and tablet — placed in participating residences.

Mental Health Triage

Structured check-ins and a clinical bridge to your university's counselling services.

Why Universities Choose CampusCare+

Healthcare infrastructure that works as hard as your students do.

01

Improved student wellbeing and attendance

Students who get care when they need it stay healthier, miss fewer classes, and perform better academically. This is measurable — and it reflects directly on institutional outcomes.

02

Clinical backup for residence staff

Sub-wardens and residence leaders gain direct clinician access for every after-hours incident. No more isolated judgement calls under pressure. Structured escalation, every time.

03

Data to plan with, not just gut feel

Anonymised, quarterly population health reports — illness patterns, mental health trends, peak periods. No other South African campus health provider offers this. Plan your wellness budget on real evidence.

04

A modern wellness offering your campus can be proud of

Students notice when their institution invests in their care. CampusCare+ is a tangible, differentiated pastoral care benefit — one that resonates in recruitment, retention, and institutional reputation.
